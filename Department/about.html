MSc Computer Science course is a 2-year postgraduate program that offer both theoretical and practical knowledge in the field of Computer Science such as software engineering, system engineering, natural computation processing, mathematical principles, and machine learning.

The program covers several key focus areas, including Advanced Algorithms, Advanced Software Engineering, and Human-Computer Interaction.
